MG @ Mini Globe Mall

Centrally located in the CBD (Central Business District) of Bangalore on Vittal Mallya Road, MG Mall is just 1.5 kms away from the MG Road – Brigade Road junction.
With a total built up area of 16 lakh sq ft, ,MG @ Mini Globe Mall is completed by the UB Tower, Kingfisher Plaza, Concorde, Canberra & Comet Blocks! Each of these blocks stand out for their distinctive look, while yet combining to produce the feeling of a city.
